About Service & Scholar

Every service member deserves a clear path to their education goals.

Service & Scholar exists to guide, motivate, and simplify the process of earning a college degree while on active duty. Whether you’re just starting to look at schools or already juggling classes with 12-hour shifts, this space is built to make the path clearer and lighter.

The focus is simple: turn your military education benefits into a degree that actually serves your future — without wasting time, money, or energy on guesswork.

The person behind the project

Service & Scholar is run by an active duty service member with over a decade in uniform and just as much time spent working through college using military education benefits.

I’ve navigated Tuition Assistance, the GI Bill, CLEP/DSST exams, and the maze of school choices from the inside. I’ve made mistakes, figured out what actually works in a busy ops environment, and built systems to stay on track between shifts, deployments, and all the “hey, got a minute?” taskers.

This site is the resource I wish I had at year one in the military: clear steps, honest expectations, and practical tools designed for real life in uniform.
