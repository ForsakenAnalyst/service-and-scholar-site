Why Service & Scholar exists

Every service member deserves a clear, realistic path to a degree that actually matters after the military.

Service & Scholar is here to cut through the confusion around schools, benefits, and schedules. The goal is simple: help you use your education benefits well, finish your degree, and still keep your sanity and performance in the job.

Built from real experience

This project is run by an active duty service member with over 10 years in uniform and just as many spent working through college using military education benefits.

I've used TA, the GI Bill, credit-by-exam, and more late-night study sessions than I can count. Everything here comes from that experience - the mistakes, the shortcuts, and the systems that actually survive real optempo.
