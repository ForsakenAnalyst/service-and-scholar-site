What is Service & Scholar?

Service & Scholar is a resource hub designed for active duty members who want to earn a college degree without burning out or getting lost in the admin maze.

If you’re juggling long shifts, weird hours, and a chain of command that doesn’t always “get it,” this is built with you in mind.

Who this is for

  • Active duty planning to start college soon
  • Already taking classes but feeling scattered
  • Trying to line up TA, GI Bill, and other funding
  • Thinking ahead to your post-service career
